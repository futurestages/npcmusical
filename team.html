BRENDAN BRADLEY
Writer-Director-Performer

Brendan Bradley is a multi-award-winning actor and creator with over 100 film and television credits and 50M+ views online. Bradley most recently appeared opposite Dolly Parton in Mountain Magic Christmas on NBC, The Daylong Brothers (opposite Keith Carradine), and in his own screenplay The Plus One (opposite Ashanti and Cedric The Entertainer). Bradley currently travels the world as the star of multiple live virtual reality projects, including Gumball Dreams, winning last year’s SXSW and Raindance; Non-Player Character premiering at this year's Edinburgh Fringe Festival; and Uncanny Alley: A New Day premiering at The Venice Film Festival. Other interactive performance capture work includes Resident Evil: Village and Marvel's Midnight Suns.

MICHAEL MORRAN
Technical Director

Michael Morran is a director and technologist from Austin, TX who is passionate about helping performing artists integrate new technologies into their creative practices. He has helped produce live VR events for dozens of clients, including Mastercard, Meta, and Mozilla, collaborating with innovative artists such as Toshi Reagon and Teflon Sega. Michael is the technical director for Non-Player Character and has designed and built a cueing system for live theatrical effects in virtual reality.

MAURICE SOQUE JR
Music Director

Maurice Soque Jr. is a musician and music producer who collaborates with artists and creatives to make amazing sounding music. Maurice's ability to weave between the artistic and technical, the musical and the mundane, has afforded him many opportunities to work with and perform for a wide variety of music and other projects, making him an essential component to many creative teams. A graduate of the Berklee College of Music, Maurice has honed his craft to become a versatile and innovative force in the world of music. With a foundation in jazz, funk, and soul music, Maurice has seamlessly transitioned to working primarily in the realms of indie and commercial pop music. He skillfully plays a wide array of instruments, including piano, guitar, bass, saxophone, brass and other woodwind instruments. This versatility allows him to bring a unique and dynamic sound to every project he undertakes.

CARON JANE LYON
UK Company Manager

Caron Jane Lyon is a producer, content creator, creative technologist and company manager. With over 25 years of experience in stagecraft, technical production, logistics, and artist relations. Caron manages immersive productions, remote teams and collaborations liaising with venues, funders and investors. Caron’s producing work spans London and Rotterdam, Rome and Reykjavik for Audiences Europe Network (AEN), Pilot Theatre and Shifthappens, RSA Augmented Society Network, Cybersalon, Royal Historical Society, Women in Architecture, Amb:IT:ion, EquityUK, DeafExplorer and TedX. Caron is Artistic Director of PCM Immersive Staging Company and is an Associate Artist with the University of Nottingham's Virtual Production Studio
